Multi-Domain Data Analytics Project 📊
📌 Project Overview
This project performs an end-to-end data analysis on three distinct datasets: Retail Sales, Real Estate, and Customer Churn. Using Python's data science ecosystem, we extract meaningful insights, identify trends, and provide business recommendations.

📂 Dataset Descriptions
Sales Data: Contains transaction records including products, regions, and revenue.

House Prices: Includes property features like area, location, and type to analyze market valuation.

Customer Churn: Focuses on telecom customer behavior to identify risk factors for service cancellation.

📈 Key Insights & Visualizations
1. Sales Performance
Top Region: North Region is the primary revenue driver.

Top Product: Laptops show the highest demand.

2. Housing Market Trends
Location Value: City Center properties are priced significantly higher than Suburb/Rural areas.

Size Correlation: A strong linear relationship exists between Area and Price.

3. Customer Churn Factors
Contract Type: High churn is observed in month-to-month contracts.

Critical Period: Customer retention efforts are most needed within the first 6 months of tenure.
